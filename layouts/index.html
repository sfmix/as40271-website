What is AS40271?

AS40271 is an opt-in cooperative delivering caching and content from major sources to local Bay Area eyeball ISPs. Rather than replacing your existing transit, AS40271 provides managed private peering with content networks — bringing popular content closer to your subscribers.

IP transit is technically available, but AS40271 is primarily built to support cache fill operations. It is not designed to be a sole-source transit replacement, and SFMIX cannot offer an SLA. Sole-sourcing your network's connectivity via SFMIX is risky and strongly discouraged.
